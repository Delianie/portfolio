ABOUT
Aus dem Auge, aus dem Sinn?
We come into this world with nothing and leave behind countless things at the end. Many objects lose their material value over time, are stored away and often forgotten – like stuffed animals in an overfilled plastic bag. Despite being forgotten, an emotional attachment remains, and letting go is difficult.
Is this overconsumption or simply the need to make space because there is a lack of storage space?
My poster visualizes precisely this tension between possessions, memories, and order.
