Identity in the Depths
What might the visual identity of a company look like that manufactures ice-diving equipment for Antarctica? The corporate design of the fictional brand Nuoto explores this question. Developed analogously using tracing paper and a set square, the resulting identity conveys cold, depth, and movement—clearly recognizable as belonging to diving equipment. The logo depicts a penguin in a diving pose: minimalistic, striking, and symbolic of reliability beneath the ice surface.
