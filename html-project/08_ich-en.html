About Me
Hey, I'm Delia, born in Switzerland in 2008 and an aspiring graphic designer who draws inspiration from sport, nature and numbers. I love design with clear systems and logical structures – from harmonious proportions to precisely constructed animations. I am particularly fascinated by how design shapes and influences our everyday lives without us even realising it. For me, good design is about translating ideas into visuals, finding solutions and combining aesthetics with function. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to send me an email :)
