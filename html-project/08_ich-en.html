About Me
Hey, I'm Delia, born in Switzerland in 2008 and an aspiring graphic designer inspired by sports, nature, and numbers. I love design with clear systems and logical structures, from harmonious proportions to precisely crafted animations. I'm especially fascinated by how design shapes and influences our everyday lives, often without us noticing it. Turning ideas into visual concepts, finding solutions, and combining aesthetics with function — that’s what good design means to me. If you have any questions or inquiries, feel free to send me an email :)
