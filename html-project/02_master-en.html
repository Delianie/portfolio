Seeing Music
How can music be made visible? My project began with simple experiments: analog on paper, digital with a camera, using liquids, and stop motion. It quickly became clear how closely music and design are connected—both rely on rhythm, tempo, and movement. This connection forms the foundation of my poster. It was created from analog scans of tracing paper, which I layered and digitally processed. A short animation expands on the idea of movement. The black-and-white aesthetic focuses everything on shapes and sequences, showing how music can be experienced as movement within the image.
