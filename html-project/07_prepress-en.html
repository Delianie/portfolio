61 Years Uldry × 61 Posters
Working with color requires courage, curiosity, and a great deal of willingness to experiment. You only discover what different color layers reveal when they overlap once you try it out yourself. In the screen-printing workshop, this process becomes possible: testing, mixing, layering, discarding, and starting again. Especially with special inks, unexpected combinations emerge that cannot be planned beforehand.

The result is a creative and multilayered poster – designed together with my entire class to celebrate the 60+1 anniversary year of Uldry AG.
